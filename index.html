Optimization of Opportunistic Networks using Particle Swarm Optimization(PSO)


Opportunistic Network

  • Opportunistic mobile social networks are a form of mobile ad hoc networks that exploit the human social characteristics, such as similarities, daily routines, mobility patterns, and interests to perform the message routing and data sharing.
  • The oppnets differ from traditional networks, in which the nodes of a single network are all deployed together, with the size of the network and locations of its nodes pre-designed.

Particle Swarm Optimization

  • Particle swarm optimization (PSO) is a method that optimizes a problem by trying to improve a candidate solution with regard to a given measure of quality.
  • This solves a problem by having a swarm of particles and moving these particles around in the search-space according to the fitness function over the particle's position and velocity.

Proposed Solution

Particle Swarm Optimization(PSO) algorithm used to establish the communication among the messages(packets) that are transferred from the mobile devices at the time of crisis. The messages are considered as particles and the particles are scattered in two dimensional space. The particles are said to move in the direction of the intermediate node, which is denoted as a target particle. With the help of PSO, the particles get an optimized route to move closer to the intermediate node.
